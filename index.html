1. New Joiner Onboarding

Welcome to the team. This section covers the fundamental security and mindset requirements for handling VIPs.

  • Security Protocol: Data protection is #1. Never share player lists outside of secure channels. Use VPN where required.
  • The KAM Mindset: You are a Sales Leader. Your job is to drive Monthly Deposit and Revenue through relationship building.
  • Daily Routine: Check previous day's VIP losses/wins at 9 AM. Reach out to top 10 players by 11 AM.

3. Module 2: Retention (Chasing Revenue)

Keeping the deposits flowing and minimizing "churn" (players leaving).

  • Deposit Chasing: If a player hasn't deposited for 48 hours, they need a "Nudge."
  • Loss Recovery: "I saw you had a rough session. I've credited a 10% loss-back as a gesture of goodwill."
  • Retention Channels: Floating (Live Chat), WhatsApp, and Telegram.
