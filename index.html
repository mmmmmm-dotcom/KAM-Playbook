Retention Strategy

Relationship building and value sustainability.

1. Purpose

VIP Retention focuses on strengthening long-term member relationships through personalized engagement, strategic value allocation, and consistent service excellence. The objective is not only to reduce churn, but to maintain long-term loyalty by ensuring members feel recognized, understood, and appropriately rewarded based on their contribution, VIP rank, and overall relationship with the platform.

Strategy Balances:

  • Member experience
  • Revenue sustainability
  • Relationship depth
  • Strategic reward allocation
  • Long-term engagement quality

Considerations:

  • VIP Rank & potential value
  • Deposit and valid bet behavior
  • ROI Funds contribution
  • Current ROI Status

Core Retention Principles

VIP RankRetention Focus
Bronze / SilverScalable engagement, responsiveness, activity stimulation
GoldRelationship building, personalized communication
PlatinumPremium service experience, loyalty reinforcement
DiamondEmotional loyalty, exclusivity, long-term relationship depth

Positive ROI Funds

Members with healthy Positive ROI Funds are sustainable contributors and may justify higher retention flexibility, strategic gifting, VIP experiences, and relationship investment for long-term value retention.

ROI Status Framework

1. Under Rewarded

Scenario: Member contribution is strong compared to historical rewards received. Approach: Increase engagement, prioritize retention before dissatisfaction develops.

2. Fairly Rewarded

Scenario: Member rewards are aligned appropriately. Approach: Maintain consistency in service quality and focus on trust.

3. Over Rewarded

Scenario: Reward allocation exceeds current contribution. Approach: Reduce dependency on incentive-driven engagement.

VIP Reactivation Strategy

Restoring valuable relationships and protecting against churn.

1. Purpose

VIP Reactivation focuses on rebuilding engagement and restoring relationships with valuable members who have become inactive or disengaged from the platform. The objective is to re-establish trust, understand inactivity reasons, and sustain healthy revenue contribution.

2. Targeted VIPs

Who to Target:

  • No deposit for 30+ days
  • Significant reduction in valid bets
  • Sudden behavioral change after losses

Priority:

  • Gold, Platinum, Diamond tiers
  • Positive ROI contributors
  • Long-term loyal members

3. Evaluation Framework

AreaConsideration
VIP RankBronze → Diamond priority handling
ROI FundsPositive revenue sustainability
ROI StatusUnder / Fairly / Over Rewarded

5. Reactivation SOP

Step 1 — Analyze Behavior Review history and ROI to understand the "Why" behind inactivity.
Step 2 — Contact Use preferred human channels (Call/WA/TG) to rebuild connection.
Step 3 — Follow-Up Monitor recovery consistency and avoid over-contacting post-recovery.

Objection Handling

🎯 Core Principle

A VIP objection is a signal. Don't just answer; diagnose and reposition.

Scenarios: "I keep losing", "Your platform hard to win"

Strategy: Acknowledge → Stabilize → Reframe
"I understand how that feels... Many players pause after a losing phase to reset."
"Support is based on overall consistency. Let me review your profile for a meaningful plan."
