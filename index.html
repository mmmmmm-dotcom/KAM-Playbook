1. Onboarding Fundamentals

Welcome to the Elite VIP Desk. As a Key Account Manager (KAM), you are the face of the company for our most valuable members.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Maintain daily contact with assigned VIPs.
  • Monitor betting behavior and potential churn.
  • Ensure 100% security of player data.

2. Acquisition: The Art of Upgrading

Identifying "Whales" hidden in the normal member list.

Acquisition Tip: Never ask for a deposit directly during the first call. Focus on "Upgrading their experience" to VIP status first.

High-Value Signals:

  • Single deposits over $500.
  • High turnover in Live Baccarat or Sports.
  • Professional/Calm tone in chat support.

3. Strategic Retention

Retention is about emotional connection. A happy player stays longer.

Retention Pillars:

Reactive: Resolving withdrawal issues or failed deposits instantly.

Proactive: Sending weekend match reminders or new game sneak peeks.

Surprise: Sending a small bonus "just because" it's their local holiday.

4. Master Objection Bank

How to flip a "No" into a "Deposit".

"The site is laggy":
"I am so sorry to hear that. I've personally reported this to our technical team for your account. While they fix it, I’ve added a $20 apology bonus for you."
